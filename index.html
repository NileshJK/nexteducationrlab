Welcome to the CuVIS tool. This tool will help you to generate a theory-informed, research-based Lesson Plan for your topic. This lesson plan will be mapped to your objective and customized to your requirements. You will proceed through a set of 'Activity Construction' prompts to generate the lesson plan.

Step 1: Select a topic whose teaching will be enhanced with visualization.

Step 2: Decide your learning objective for teaching with visualization.

Step 3: Select your visualization mapped to objective.

Step 4: Decide which active learning strategy to use for your activity with visualization.

Step 5: Decide your activity with visualization that is Constructively aligned.

Step 6: Note where to pause visualization to start activity.

Step 7: Design the lesson plan based on your decisions in steps 1-6.

Learning Objectives with visualization

Select the learning objective that best matches with your objective.To view example of each objective roll mouse over the text.

Visualize to explain a concept with illustration.
Visualize to explain the working of a process/ algorithm OR, Compare multiple processes.
Write/Draw alternate representations (like graph to equation) from the given visualization or vice-versa.
Use a given visualization to compute the solution to the given problem involving multiple processes.
Predict output of next step or a set or a set of steps in a multi-process.
Predict output of a phenomenon.
Students devise an explanation for a given process or phenomena, through logical reasoning, from observations made from the visualization, before they have been taught the topic.
